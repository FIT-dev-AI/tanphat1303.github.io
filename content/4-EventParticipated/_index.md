During my internship and participation in learning activities within the AWS community, I had the opportunity to attend an event focused on enterprise cloud architecture, cloud computing trends, and the practical application of Generative AI in modern business environments.

The event brought together speakers from AWS Vietnam and its partners, providing insights into large-scale data systems, cloud engineering career paths, and the role of cloud architecture in solving real-world business challenges. It also helped me better understand the skills required for future work in Cloud, Data, and AI-related roles.

### [Event 1 - Cloud, AI Agent, and Amazon Q on AWS](4.1-event1/)

&emsp;**Event Name:** Cloud, AI Agent, and Amazon Q on AWS

&emsp;**Time:** June 27, 2026

&emsp;**Location:** 26th Floor, Bitexco Building, No. 3 Hai Trieu Street, Sai Gon Ward, Ho Chi Minh City

&emsp;**Role in the Event:** Participant

&emsp;**Main Content:** This event covered AI Agent applications on AWS, including building a Voice Agent, automating incident handling with a DevOps AI Agent, applying Amazon Q in human resource management, and securing enterprise AI applications with Private Connection. The sessions helped me understand how Cloud, AI, DevOps, and security work together to build modern business systems.

> Overall, the event gave me useful insights into how AWS supports AI automation and secure enterprise workflows.

### [Event 2 - AWS Enterprise Cloud Architectures & Industry Applications](4.2-event2/)

&emsp;**Event Name:** AWS – Enterprise Cloud Architectures & Industry Applications

&emsp;**Time:** July 04, 2026

&emsp;**Location:** 26th Floor, Bitexco Financial Tower, 2 Hai Trieu Street, Sai Gon Ward, Ho Chi Minh City

&emsp;**Role in the Event:** Participant

&emsp;**Main Content:** The event focused on introducing enterprise cloud architecture, the development of Cloud Computing and Generative AI, and the demand for cloud talent in the industry. The program also included a discussion on the development of technology talent in Vietnam, helping me better understand the role of cloud architecture, problem-solving skills, and practical experience in the business environment.

> Overall, this event gave me a clearer view of the importance of cloud architecture, problem-solving thinking, and practical technical skills in enterprise environments.
